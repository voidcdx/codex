Mods (in slot order)

Mods apply in slot order — top-left first. Slot order determines elemental combination: the first two primary elements (Heat, Cold, Electricity, Toxin) encountered combine into a secondary (e.g. Cold + Toxin = Viral, Heat + Electricity = Radiation). Innate weapon elements occupy the last slot.

Primed, Umbral, and Archon variants block their base version — only one variant per mod family can be equipped at a time.

Click the purple slot to open the Riven Builder. Use the gold alchemy button to explore elemental combinations.

Weapon Arcanes

Weapon arcanes slot into a max-rank built weapon and use no mod capacity. Up to 2 arcane slots per weapon.

Merciless: Stacks on kill — up to +180% damage and −90% reload at 10 stacks.

Deadhead: Stacks on headshot kill — up to +120% headshot bonus and −60% reload at 10 stacks.

Cascadia Flare / Empowered: Proc on status hit, granting temporary crit chance or crit damage.

Cascadia Overcharge: On reload, adds flat IPS damage distributed proportionally across Impact, Puncture, and Slash.

The stack count you enter represents active stacks at the moment of this hit.

Quick Start

  1. Search and select a Weapon in the top-left panel.
  2. Equip mods by clicking any mod card slot. Drag cards to reorder them.
  3. Search and select an Enemy. Set its level, Steel Path, and Eximus state if needed.
  4. Adjust Options (crit mode, body part, status stacks, etc.).
  5. Click Calculate. Results appear on the right.

Weapon Selector

Type any part of a weapon name to search. Exalted weapons and Garuda Talons are hidden.

  • Attack tabs — weapons with multiple fire modes (e.g. Acceltra Prime's Rocket Impact vs Explosion, Torid's Grenade vs Cloud) show tabs below the weapon name. Each tab has its own damage, crit, status, and fire rate.
  • Kuva / Tenet bonus element — a bonus element row appears automatically for Kuva and Tenet weapons. Pick the element type (Heat / Cold / Electricity / Toxin) and the bonus percentage (25–60%). The bonus is treated as an innate secondary element and combines with mod elements using HCET priority.

Mod Slots

The mod panel has up to 10 slots: 8 regular + 1 Stance (melee only) + 1 Exilus.

SlotWhat goes there
Regular (×8)Any damage, elemental, IPS, crit, multishot, fire rate, reload, magazine, status, or faction mod compatible with the weapon type.
Stance ⚔Melee stance mods only (shown for melee weapons). Provides combo multiplier bonuses and attack combos.
Exilus ✦Utility mods: ammo drum, mutation, magazine, air recon, etc. The list is weapon-type-specific. Weapon-specific exilus mods (e.g. Ambush Optics for Rubico) only appear when that weapon is selected.
Riven ◆Built via the Riven Builder (see below). Purple diamond card. Stats are fully custom.

Drag mod cards left or right to reorder them. Slot order controls elemental combination priority — the top-left slot pairs with the next available element first.

Elemental Combining

Four primary elements (Heat, Cold, Electricity, Toxin) combine in mod-slot order to produce combined elements. Visual arc lines and badges below the mod grid show the active combos live.

CombinedRecipe

CorrosiveElectricity + Toxin

A weapon's innate primary elements slot in after all mod slots (slot 9). Exception: Kuva/Tenet innate elements follow HCET priority (Heat → Cold → Electricity → Toxin) and merge with a matching mod slot if one exists. Secondary (combined) innate elements (e.g. Magnetic on Kuva Nukor) are a fixed bucket — they cannot be further combined.

Riven Builder

Click the + button in the mod panel header to open the Riven Builder.

  • Up to 4 stat rows. Each row: pick a stat from the dropdown, enter a % value (negative for penalties, e.g. −50 for Recoil).
  • Click Add to equip the riven. The purple diamond card appears in the mod grid and shows each stat.
  • Click the card again (or re-open the builder) to edit. Reset clears all rows.
  • Available stats: Damage, Multishot, Crit Chance, Crit Damage, Status Chance, Fire Rate, Heat, Cold, Electricity, Toxin, Impact, Puncture, Slash.

Alchemy Mixer

Click the gold seal icon in the mod panel header to open the Alchemy Mixer — a visual tool for exploring elemental combinations.

  • Select two primary elements. An animated merge shows the result with its color and name.
  • Below the animation, compatible elemental mods for the selected weapon type are listed as chips.
  • Equip These Mods fills empty mod slots with the suggested mods.

Hit Options

OptionRangeWhat it does
Crit ModeAverage / Guaranteed / MaxAverage — weighted DPS average across all crit tiers (best for build comparison). Guaranteed — floor tier, worst-case per hit. Max — ceiling tier, best-case per hit.
Body PartPopulated from enemy dataApplies the body part damage multiplier (Step 2 of the pipeline). Head is typically ×2. On a crit + headshot, the crit multiplier is also doubled.
Viral Stacks0–10Viral status stacks on the enemy. Multiplies final damage: 1 stack = ×1.75, up to 10 stacks = ×4.25.
Corrosive Stacks0–10Corrosive status stacks strip enemy armor. Each stack removes ~6% (formula: min(80%, 20% + 6%×N)).
Combo Counter1–12 (Venka Prime: 1–13)Melee only. Sets the melee combo tier. Tier 1 = ×1.0, Tier 2 = ×2.0, …
Active Statuses0–10Unique status types currently on the enemy. Used by Condition Overload (+80% damage per unique status) and Galvanized Aptitude.
Galvanized Stacks0–5Kill stacks for Galvanized mods (e.g. Galvanized Chamber, Savvy, Crosshairs). Only shown when a Galvanized mod is equipped. Each mod has its own cap (4–5 stacks).

Enemy Panel

  • Level (1–9999) — scales enemy health, shield, armor, and overguard using the faction-specific smoothstep formulas from the wiki. The live stat cards update instantly.
  • Steel Path — applies ×2.5 to health and shields (not level). Does not affect armor or overguard scaling.
  • Eximus — enables Overguard scaling. The Overguard card appears in the live stat display.
  • Scaled stats cards — show the final HP, Shield, Armor, and Overguard at the current level and settings, formatted with thousands separators.

Reading Results

After clicking Calculate, the results panel shows:

  • Total damage — final per-hit damage (after all 6 pipeline steps, including multishot).
  • Breakdown table — damage per type (Impact, Puncture, Slash, elementals). +50% badges = enemy is vulnerable to that type. −50% badges = enemy is resistant. These are faction-based (Update 36+).
  • Status Procs table — if the weapon has elemental damage, shows per-tick and 6-tick total for DoT procs (Slash bleed, Heat burn, Gas cloud, Toxin poison, Electricity arc). Faction mods double-dip on these.
  • CC / Debuff Procs table — shows crowd-control effects for Viral (health vulnerability), Magnetic (shield damage), Radiation (confuse), Blast (accuracy debuff), Cold (slow).
  • Condition Overload Curve — when a Condition Overload mod is equipped, shows a table of damage at 0–10 unique statuses with bar chart and % increase over baseline. The current status count row is highlighted.
  • DPS section — Burst DPS (damage × fire rate), Sustained DPS (accounts for reload), and Proc DPS (steady-state average from status effects).
  • Kill Time section — shots to kill, burst TTK, and sustained TTK based on the scaled enemy HP at the selected level.

Results

Damage Breakdown: Per-trigger damage after all 6 pipeline steps (base → mods → body part → type effectiveness → armor → faction). Each row is a damage type. The +50% / −50% badges show faction effectiveness (vulnerable or resistant) against the selected enemy.

Status Procs: DoT procs (Slash, Heat, Gas, Toxin, Electricity) deal damage over 6 ticks (~7s). Faction mods double-dip — the proc inherits the bonus from the triggering hit and applies it again each tick. CC/Debuff procs (Viral, Magnetic, Radiation, Blast, Cold) show their crowd-control effect only — no tick damage.

Burst DPS: Damage per trigger × fire rate × multishot — assumes infinite magazine with no reload downtime.

Sustained DPS: Accounts for reload time. This is the realistic long-run DPS including the time spent reloading.

Shots to Kill / TTK: Calculated against the selected enemy's scaled health, shields, and overguard at the chosen level, with the modelled armor already factored in.

Select a weapon and enemy, then click Calculate.

Options

Hit Type: Average uses the mathematical expected value — best for DPS. Guaranteed Crit forces every shot to crit at the base tier. Max Crit forces the highest possible tier (e.g. 350% CC → Tier 3).

Viral / Corrosive / Cold Stacks: Status effects already on the enemy. Viral multiplies health damage (×1.75 at 1 stack → ×4.25 at 10). Corrosive strips 26% armor on first stack, +6% each after (max 80%). Cold adds +0.1 flat to the crit damage multiplier per stack.

Combo (melee only): Combo tier (1–12). Affects Blood Rush, Weeping Wounds, and combo-scaling mods.

Active Status: Unique proc types on the enemy — used by Condition Overload (+80% per type) and Galvanized Aptitude.

Galv. Stacks: Active kill-stacks on equipped Galvanized mods. Earned by kills, reset on mission start. Each mod has its own cap (typically 4–5).

Armor Strip: Set Ability Strip % and Corrosive Projection % below to see remaining armor and damage reduction before calculating. Strips additively, capped at 100%.

Warframe Buffs

Roar (Rhino/subsumed): Adds to faction mods — e.g. Roar 50% + Bane 30% = ×1.80. Double-dips on DoT procs (Slash bleed, Heat burn, Gas cloud).

Eclipse (Mirage/subsumed): Separate multiplicative step after faction. Does not double-dip on procs.

Xata's Whisper (Xaku/subsumed): Separate Void hit — double-dips faction bonus and headshot multiplier.

Nourish (Grendel/subsumed): Adds Viral elemental damage proportional to base damage.

Enter ability strength as a percentage (e.g. 150 for 150% strength).

Armor Strip

Ability Strip %: Armor removed by a Warframe ability (e.g. 100% for max-strength Terrify, 50% for Breach Surge). Check the ability's description for the exact value.

Corrosive Projection: Aura mod that strips a flat % of base armor — stacks additively with ability strip. Per-player: 18% → 36% → 54% → 72% at full squad.

Order of operations: Ability % + CP % (additive, capped at 100%) → Corrosive proc stacks from Options. Void and True damage bypass armor entirely.
